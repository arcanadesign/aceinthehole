ABOUT US

Headquartered in beautiful Portland, Oregon, Ace in the Hole Multiport Events is an event company that hosts multisport athletic events. Ace is proud to offer running and triathlon events to athletes of all shapes and sizes, national origins, sexual orientations and cultural backgrounds. We offer Events for Every Body.

Ace events cater to every level of athletic ability, including a first timer triathlon, a sprint, Olympic, and Half-Iron triathlons and 10K and Half marathon runs. Come to experience your first race or come to compete to win, but make sure you come to have fun!

We are the proud host of the Annual Ace in the Hole MultiSport Weekend, a legendary event in the Oregon triathlon and running community. It has become a traditional destination race for athletes from across the nation.
